THE WORLD YOU MUST ENDURE

Start with limited resources and survive long enough to escape or rebuild a collapsing nation.

about

The nation once promised stability and progress. Years of unchecked inflation and corrupt leadership shattered that promise. Money lost its value. Trust disappeared. Streets filled with unrest and quiet fear.

You are an ordinary citizen left behind by the system. You begin with a small stash of resources and no protection from the chaos around you. Each day forces you to choose between survival and change. Obey, resist, escape, or rebuild. Time moves forward no matter what you decide.

Your story ends in one of two ways. You flee the country before it collapses completely. Or you endure long enough to reshape it into something better. Health fades. Freedom shrinks. The clock never stops.

core mechanics

survival

Your hunger decreases each day and drops faster when you travel, work, protest, or fight. Low hunger reduces your health recovery, action efficiency, and chance of success. If hunger reaches zero, you lose health and face higher risks like illness or arrest. You must decide whether to buy overpriced food, scavenge with danger, or endure hunger to save resources for escape or reform.

action choice

You receive a limited number of action points daily. Every action consumes time, hunger, and sometimes freedom or safety. Some actions provide short term survival while others push long term goals. Your choices trigger consequences that can appear immediately or days later. Each day forces you to choose between staying safe and taking risks to change your future.

farming

You can plant small gardens or community farms using limited resources. Crops take several days to grow and depend on security and random events. Successful harvests provide food and trade goods. Farming reduces hunger pressure over time but leaves you vulnerable to raids or government seizure. You must decide whether to protect, share, or exploit your food supply.
