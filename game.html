THE WORLD YOU MUST ENDURE

Start with limited resources and survive long enough to escape or rebuild a collapsing nation.

about

The nation once promised stability and progress. Years of unchecked inflation and corrupt leadership shattered that promise. Money lost its value. Trust disappeared. Streets filled with unrest and quiet fear.

You are an ordinary citizen left behind by the system. You begin with a small stash of resources and no protection from the chaos around you. Each day forces you to choose between survival and change. Obey, resist, escape, or rebuild. Time moves forward no matter what you decide.

Your story ends in one of two ways. You flee the country before it collapses completely. Or you endure long enough to reshape it into something better. Health fades. Freedom shrinks. The clock never stops.
